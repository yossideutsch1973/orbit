FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Orbit uses data-driven system identification to learn a mathematical model of your system's dynamics. No equations needed — just measurement data. Under the hood, it builds a Koopman operator model that captures both linear and nonlinear behavior from your time-series data.

For linear systems (most circuits, vibration problems, control systems), Orbit achieves machine-precision accuracy (RMSE < 10-10). For nonlinear systems, accuracy is excellent for short-to-medium prediction horizons, with calibrated confidence bands so you always know how much to trust.
